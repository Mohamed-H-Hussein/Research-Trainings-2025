# 🧬 African BioGenome Project (AfricaBP, 2025) — Marine Bioinformatics Hackathon: Biodiversity, Genomics & Nextflow Pipelines

## Overview

This folder contains **personal training notes and scientific learning summaries** from the **Marine Bioinformatics Hackathon (AfricaBP 2025)** held at **Nile University (29–31 October 2025)**.

The workshop combined **lectures, hands-on practical sessions, and collaborative hackathon projects** focusing on marine biodiversity genomics and metagenomic data analysis.

The training covered:

* Marine biodiversity genomics and conservation
* Metagenomics analysis of microbial communities
* 16S rRNA amplicon sequencing workflows
* Bioinformatics analysis using the **Galaxy platform**
* Taxonomic classification and visualization of microbial communities
* Workflow automation and reproducible pipelines using **Nextflow**
* Exploration of **nf-core standardized bioinformatics pipelines**

These notes document the **scientific concepts, tools, and analytical workflows** explored during the three-day training.

## Skills Developed

**Day 1 — Marine Genomics & Metagenomics Foundations**

* Understanding threats to marine biodiversity and conservation genomics
* Overview of next-generation sequencing technologies
* Introduction to metagenomics and marine drug discovery
* Practical experience using the **Galaxy bioinformatics platform**
* Preprocessing of **16S rRNA amplicon sequencing data**
* Sequence dereplication, filtering, and alignment using **mothur tools**
* Preparation of sequencing data for downstream microbial diversity analysis

---

**Day 2 — Taxonomic Analysis & Visualization**

* Extraction of taxonomic information from processed sequencing datasets
* Understanding **OTU clustering and microbial diversity estimation**
* Using **reference taxonomic databases** for classification
* Building **OTU abundance tables** representing microbial community composition
* Visualization of microbial communities using:
  * **Krona interactive taxonomy charts**
  * **Phyloseq (R) visualizations**
  * **MultiQC analysis summaries**
* Interpreting biodiversity patterns in environmental metagenomic datasets

---

**Day 3 — Workflow Automation & Reproducible Bioinformatics**

* Understanding the role of **workflow managers in bioinformatics**
* Building reproducible pipelines using **Nextflow**
* Learning core Nextflow concepts:
  * Processes
  * Channels
  * Workflows
  * Configuration files
* Using **containerized environments (Docker / Singularity)** for reproducibility
* Exploring **nf-core community pipelines**
* Collaborative development of bioinformatics workflows during the hackathon project

---
