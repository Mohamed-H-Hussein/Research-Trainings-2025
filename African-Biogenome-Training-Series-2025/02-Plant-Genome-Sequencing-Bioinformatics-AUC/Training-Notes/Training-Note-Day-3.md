### Session 3: MiSeq Overview   

**Type:** Lecture / Practical Overview     
**Description:** Learn theoretical principles of MiSeq sequencing, instrument layout, and run configurations.

* **MiSeq i100 Series Features:**

  * Fast, simplified benchtop sequencing
  * Same-day results (as fast as 4 hours)
  * Onboard cluster generation and secondary analysis
  * Multiple reagent kits for flexible read lengths and outputs

* **Reagent Kit Components:**

  * Box 1: Reagent Cartridge, HT1 Buffer (store -25°C to -15°C)
  * Box 2: Flow Cell, PR2 Buffer (store 2°C to 8°C)

* **Output Options:**

  * V2 Kits: 50–500 cycles, 850 Mb–8.5 Gb, up to 15M reads
  * V3 Kits: 150–600 cycles, 3.8–15 Gb, up to 25M reads
  * i100 Plus System: Max 30 Gb output, 100M reads, supports 100 small genomes/run

* **Sustainability & Ease-of-Use:**

  * RT shipping & storage, reduced packaging & carbon footprint
  * Minimal hands-on time, optimized for non-expert labs
  * Prebuilt DRAGEN pipelines simplify data analysis
