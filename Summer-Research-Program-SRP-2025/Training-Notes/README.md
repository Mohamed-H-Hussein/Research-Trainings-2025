## Skills Developed

* **Molecular Biology & Post-Transcriptional Regulation**

  * mRNA stability, translation, decay pathways
  * RNA-binding proteins and Ccr4-Not complex
  * miRNA-mediated gene regulation

* **Virology & Host-Pathogen Interactions**

  * Viral replication, cap-snatching, polymerase activity
  * Host factors affecting viral pathogenesis
  * Influenza A and SARS-CoV-2 comparative mechanisms

* **Genetically Modified Mouse Models**

  * Forward and reverse genetics approaches
  * Tissue-specific gene manipulation using CRISPR-Cas9
  * Linking genotype to phenotype for functional studies

* **Bacterial Cell Biology & Stress Response**

  * Nucleoid structure, DNA protection under oxidative stress
  * Minor subpopulation gene expression and antibiotic resistance
  * Integration with global gene regulation and infection biology

* **Lipid Biology & Metabolic Regulation**

  * Fatty acid elongation, saturation patterns, chain-length effects
  * Elovl6 and ALCAT1 roles in mitochondria and disease
  * Lipid-based therapeutic strategies

* **Medical Physics & Radiotherapy**

  * Radiation principles, imaging modalities (X-ray, CT, PET, PET-CT fusion)
  * Targeted radiotherapy planning, ALARA compliance
  * Proton therapy and dosimetry

* **Neuroscience & Sleep Biology**

  * Forward genetics in mice for sleep trait discovery
  * Orexin signaling, REM/NREM regulation
  * EEG-based sleep studies, translational applications

* **Oncoimmunology & Single-Cell Analysis**

  * Tumor microenvironment profiling using scRNA-seq
  * Clonal hematopoiesis, immune evasion, therapeutic responses
  * Integration of omics data

* **Cancer Biology & Pathology**

  * Lung neuroendocrine carcinoma classification, YAP1-positive populations
  * Experimental modeling with cell lines and tissue specimens
  * Integration of histology, immunostaining, molecular profiling

* **Space Biology & Multi-Omics**

  * cfRNA/cfDNA extraction and sequencing for longitudinal studies
  * Multi-omics integration: transcriptomics, epigenomics, small RNA profiling
  * Robotic automation (Labdroid Maholo), microgravity-induced molecular changes

* **Transcription Factor Biology & Developmental Genetics**

  * Functional characterization of Maf family transcription factors
  * CRISPR-Cas9 genome-edited mice
  * Organ-specific phenotyping and transcriptome analysis

* **Computational Drug Discovery**

  * Ligand- and structure-based drug design
  * Molecular docking, metadynamics, AI-driven compound design
  * Predictive modeling and experimental validation

* **Neuroscience & Sleep Research**

  * Electrophysiology and optogenetics in rodents
  * Neural network dynamics during slow-wave sleep
  * Synaptic homeostasis, memory consolidation

* **Experimental Design & Technical Competence**

  * Study planning across cell lines, animal models, and human specimens
  * Tissue collection, library preparation, imaging, electrophysiology
  * Data QC, multi-omics integration, computational analysis

* **Broader Scientific Competencies**

  * Integration of molecular, cellular, and computational approaches
  * Interdisciplinary problem-solving across multiple biomedical fields
  * Emphasis on experimental rigor, reproducibility, and translational relevance
