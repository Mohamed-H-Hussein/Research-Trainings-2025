# 📘 Training Overview

The winter school focused on emerging technologies and computational methods used in **modern cancer research and precision medicine**, including:

* **Precision oncology and genomic medicine**
* Integration of **bioinformatics, artificial intelligence, and biomedical engineering**
* **Optical biosensors and lab-on-chip diagnostic technologies**
* **AI-driven medical imaging and diagnostic workflows**
* **Multi-omics data integration in complex disease research**
* **Computational drug discovery and AI-assisted target identification**
* **Single-cell RNA sequencing (scRNA-seq) analysis workflows**
* **Spatial transcriptomics and tumor microenvironment analysis**
* Emerging technologies such as **virtual reality, digital twins, and AI-based healthcare monitoring systems**

The program combined **scientific lectures, technical workshops, and laboratory demonstrations**, providing interdisciplinary exposure to both **computational and technological innovations in biomedical research**.

# 🎯 Relevance to My Research Field

As an MSc candidate in **Biochemistry and Molecular Biology**, specializing in **Molecular Cancer Biology and Bioinformatics**, this winter school provided interdisciplinary exposure to computational and technological approaches relevant to modern cancer research.

Key learning areas included:

* **Single-cell transcriptomics analysis** for studying tumor heterogeneity
* **Multi-omics integration** in complex disease research
* **AI-driven computational biology and drug discovery**
* **Precision oncology and genomic diagnostics**
* Emerging biomedical technologies such as **optical biosensors and AI-assisted healthcare systems**

The training strengthened my ability to connect **computational bioinformatics methods with translational biomedical research**.
